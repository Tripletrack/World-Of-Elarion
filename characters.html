Characters of Elarion

Meet the heroes, villains, and curious souls that shape this magical world.

Omer

A half-orc, half-human druid with a deep connection to nature and his loyal dog companion, Bagira. Omer wields natural magic and often finds himself caught between instinct and duty.

Sam

An elven ranger with a steady aim and two black-and-white cat companions. Sam walks softly and carries sharp arrows. He keeps to the wild paths and rarely speaks — unless it's important.

Aviv

A steadfast human paladin clad in shining armor. Aviv prays for divine strength and carries a heavy sword — and a heavier sense of justice. Faith is his weapon and his shield.

Asaf

A lizardfolk bard who plays drums and causes trouble wherever he goes. Asaf's rhythm carries magic — and mischief. Loud, charming, and impossible to contain.

Hila

A half-elf rogue with quick hands and quicker judgment. Hila is always two steps ahead and four steps into trouble. Shadows are her element, and secrets her trade.
